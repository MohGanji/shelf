eLibrary

Here I keep a list of books I loved reading, and will probably read again. It will also contain my notes, basically what I learned from them, how they affected me and how I felt about them.

The reason for this eLibrary is that I tend to read very slowly. So I need to have a way to choose high quality books. What helps me most in cherry-picking good books is through the book notes in blogs of people who I follow regularly. Since those people inspire me, their book notes basically lead me to books that shaped their mindsets, so they have a high chance of being good books.

I'm trying to create my own library, to first spread the words of those books, and also share my learning experience from them.
