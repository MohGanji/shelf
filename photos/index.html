Photography

I'm often fascinated by how photographers find frames of beauty in what others take for granted. I think its a matter of perspective, of accepting a limitation that all I can't fit the whole world in my frame, so I have to choose.

I don't have a camera, and I'm not a professional photographer. I use my phone and try to share the tiniest bit of beauty I observe in my daily life.

For now, they are all in this Instagram page: instagram.com/iphonogr.am

I may one day decide to compile them here in this website since instagram is an external service and it may seize to exist some day.
