Tech Blog

This is a tech blog, here I will share things I learn from my work environments.

A little about me: I have a bachelors of computer science. During that time, I started working at a startup part-time. There I had the chance to try a little bit of everything - backend development, front-end development, UX, scrum, mentorship, designing projects from scratch, etc. That startup failed.

Then I started to work at a bigger company, there I was part of a larger community. I had to communicate with people from sales, design, product managers, etc.

Then I quit my job and started studying my master's. Doing research was a completely different story than working in the tech industry. I didn't even know how and what to report to my supervisor.

During all these times, I tried to take notes of learning curves and my failures - "things I wish I knew before I got into this". Here I write about those things.
